EMPIRE LIGHTS

Empire Lights is a personal project in concept to honor the iconic Empire State Building lighting show that happens everyday in New York City.

I was walking on the street around Flatiron the other day. I raised my head and saw the magnificent Empire State Building was lit with green, blue, and sparkling white lights. I was so delighted and wanted to find out what the lightings meant.

Soon I was disappointed by the short description of "Green and blue in honor of Empire State Realty Trust" on the Empire State Building's website.

I wanted to know more why it was green and blue. At least a logo of the company would help. Therefore I decided to enhance this experience by coming up with a concept with just simple color blocks that explain the meanings of each color.
