About Yaya
ENTER THE WORMHOLE

I grew up in a beautiful city in Taiwan, called Tainan. The city's optimistic vibe has a strong influence on my cheerful personality.

When I was in 9th grade, I flew to America to attend high school in New Jersey.

In 2012, I graduated from the Industrial and Interaction Design program at Syracuse University, and moved to New York City.

After working for a year, I decided to take the UXDi course at General Assembly to focus on User Experience design.
