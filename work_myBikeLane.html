THIS IS MY BIKE LANE

This is a personal project in concept created after my frustration biking in New York City.

TOO MANY CARS ARE PARKING ON MY BIKE LANES!

Even NYPD Officers are parking on my bike lane.

I posted this image on my Twitter, and immediately many people retweeted with the same frustration.

As a User Experience designer, I believe this is the time for me to contribute and address such issue. I came up with this concept of posting graphic sticky notes on cars who park on bike lanes and hoping it will raise the awareness of bicyclists' safety as well as to educate car drivers (since obviously the regulation is not being enforced by NYPD).

The side facing inside of the car writes This is my bike lane with a link to the website at mybikelane.nyc. Interestingly someone had already registered the mybikelane.com domain which allowed people to post photos regarding the same issue but unfortunately the site is now shut down. A very similar project is also happening in Toronto.

This Is My Bike Lane will not only be a personal project, but should become everyone's action.
